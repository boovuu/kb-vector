# Allowlist and Denylist Settings

> Source: https://learn.vector.co/articles/9526804207-allowlist-and-denylist-settings

---

Vector's Allowlist and Denylist settings give you control over which traffic is tracked and which is excluded.

## Denylist
Use the denylist to exclude traffic you don't want to appear in your Visitor Feed or trigger notifications.

### What You Can Denylist
- **IP addresses** — exclude specific IPs (e.g., your office IP, known bot IPs)
- **Email domains** — exclude contacts from specific companies (e.g., your own company domain)
- **Companies** — exclude specific company names from your results

### How to Add to Denylist
1. Go to Settings → Allowlist/Denylist
2. Select the Denylist tab
3. Enter the IP address, email domain, or company name to block
4. Click Add

## Allowlist
The allowlist ensures only traffic from approved sources is surfaced. This is useful if you want to limit Vector to specific domains or traffic sources.

### Common Use Cases
- Exclude your own team's traffic by adding your office IP to the denylist
- Exclude competitor companies from your feed
- Exclude known bot and crawler IP ranges

## Tips
- Adding your company's own domain to the denylist is recommended to keep internal traffic out of your feed
- Check this list if you notice unexpected contacts appearing or are missing contacts you'd expect to see
