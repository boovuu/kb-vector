# Building Your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

> Source: https://learn.vector.co/articles/3005648389-building-your-ideal-customer-profile-icp

---

Vector helps you identify and prioritize visitors who match your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) using firmographic and behavioral filters.

## What Is an ICP Segment?
An ICP segment in Vector automatically filters your visitor feed to show only contacts who match your target customer criteria. Instead of reviewing all visitors, you see only the ones worth reaching out to.

## Common ICP Filters

### Firmographic Filters
- **Company size**: e.g., 100-1000 employees
- **Industry**: e.g., SaaS, Financial Services, Healthcare
- **Geography**: e.g., United States, North America
- **Company revenue**: e.g., $10M-$100M ARR
- **Technology stack**: e.g., uses Salesforce, HubSpot

### Contact-Level Filters
- **Job title**: e.g., contains "VP", "Director", "Head of"
- **Department**: e.g., Marketing, Sales, IT
- **Seniority**: e.g., Manager and above

### Behavioral Filters
- **Pages visited**: e.g., visited /pricing or /demo
- **Number of visits**: e.g., visited 3+ times in 30 days
- **Time on site**: e.g., spent more than 2 minutes

## How to Build an ICP Segment
1. Go to Segments → New Segment
2. Name it something clear like "ICP Visitors - Tier 1"
3. Add firmographic filters matching your target company criteria
4. Add contact-level filters for the right personas
5. Optionally add behavioral filters for higher-intent signals
6. Turn on the segment
7. Add a notification action (Slack, email, or webhook)

## Tips
- Start with broader criteria and narrow down based on results
- Create tiered ICP segments (Tier 1, Tier 2) for different notification urgency levels
- Review your segment match rate weekly and adjust filters if too narrow or too broad
