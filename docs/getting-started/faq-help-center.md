Frequently asked questions about the Vector platform.

## General

**What is Vector?**
Vector is a B2B intent data and site de-anonymization platform. It identifies the companies and individual contacts visiting your website and surfaces buying intent signals, enabling your sales and marketing teams to engage prospects at the right moment.

**How does Vector identify visitors?**
Vector uses a combination of device fingerprinting and a proprietary identity graph to match anonymous website visitors to known contact profiles.

**What percentage of my traffic will Vector identify?**
Typically 10-30% of B2B website traffic. Identification rates are higher for U.S. traffic and desktop visitors.

## Pixel & Tracking

**Will the pixel slow down my website?**
No. The Vector pixel loads asynchronously and has no measurable impact on page load time.

**Does Vector work with SPAs (Single Page Applications)?**
Yes. The pixel tracks navigation events in React, Vue, Angular, and other SPA frameworks.

## Data & Privacy

**Is Vector GDPR compliant?**
Vector is designed to operate within applicable privacy laws including GDPR and CCPA. The pixel respects browser privacy signals such as Global Privacy Control (GPC).

**How long is data retained?**
Contact activity data is retained for 13 months by default.

## Integrations

**Which CRMs does Vector integrate with?**
Vector has native integrations with HubSpot and Salesforce. Other CRMs can be connected via webhook and Zapier.

**Which ad platforms does Vector support?**
LinkedIn Ads, Google Ads, Meta Ads, Reddit Ads, X Ads (Twitter), Snapchat Ads, and TikTok Ads.
