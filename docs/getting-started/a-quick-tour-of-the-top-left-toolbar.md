# A Quick Tour of the Top Left Toolbar

> Source: https://learn.vector.co/articles/6464385149-a-quick-tour-of-the-top-left-toolbar

---

The top left toolbar in Vector provides quick access to the platform's main sections.

## Toolbar Items

### Visitor Feed
The main view showing real-time identified visitors to your site. Filter, search, and take action on individual contacts.

### Segments
Create and manage your audience segments. Each segment defines a set of criteria and triggers actions (Slack notifications, webhooks, CRM syncs, ad platform audience exports) when contacts match.

### Account Lists
Upload and manage your target account lists. Use these lists as filters in your segments.

### Campaign Reporting
View attribution data for your paid ad campaigns. See which contacts came from which campaigns and track pipeline influenced by paid channels.

### Integrations
Connect and manage all your integrations — CRMs (HubSpot, Salesforce), ad platforms (LinkedIn, Google, Meta, etc.), communication tools (Slack), and webhooks.

### Settings
Manage your account settings, pixel configuration, team members, billing, and API keys.

## Navigation Tips
- Use keyboard shortcut G then V to jump to the Visitor Feed
- Use G then S to jump to Segments
- The notification bell in the top right shows recent segment matches
- Your workspace name and account switcher are in the top left corner
