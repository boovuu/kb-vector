# The New Visitor Feed

> Source: https://learn.vector.co/articles/9647950202-the-new-visitor-feed

---

The Visitor Feed is Vector's real-time view of identified contacts visiting your website. The redesigned Visitor Feed makes it easier to spot the most relevant visitors and take action.

## What's in the Visitor Feed
For each identified visitor, the feed shows:
- Contact name and photo (from LinkedIn)
- Job title and company
- Company size, industry, and location
- Pages visited in this session
- Time of visit
- Segment membership badges

## Filtering the Feed
Filter the Visitor Feed to focus on what matters:
- **Segment filter**: Show only visitors in a specific segment
- **Company filter**: Search for a specific company
- **Date range**: View visitors from a specific time period
- **Job title filter**: Show only contacts with specific titles

## Taking Action from the Feed
From any visitor card, you can:
- Click to see their full activity history
- Open their LinkedIn profile
- Add them to a HubSpot sequence (if integrated)
- Mark as reviewed

## Real-Time Updates
The feed updates in real time — new visitors appear at the top as they're identified. Enable browser notifications to get alerted when new visitors appear.

## List View vs. Card View
Toggle between a compact list view (good for scanning many visitors quickly) and a card view (good for reviewing individual profiles in detail).
