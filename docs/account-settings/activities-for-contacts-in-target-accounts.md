# Activities for Contacts in Target Accounts

> Source: https://learn.vector.co/articles/4247249786-activities-for-contacts-in-target-accounts

---

Vector surfaces individual contact-level activity for visitors from your target accounts, giving you granular insight into who at a target company is researching your product.

## What You Can See
For each contact from a target account who visits your site, Vector shows:
- Contact name, title, and LinkedIn URL
- Pages they visited and time spent
- When they first visited and most recently visited
- Which segment(s) they match
- Their intent signals

## Setting Up Target Account Lists
See the Account Lists article for instructions on uploading your target account list.

## Viewing Contact Activity
1. Go to the Visitor Feed in Vector
2. Filter by Segment or use the search to find a specific company
3. Click on a contact to see their full activity timeline

## In HubSpot
If you have the HubSpot integration connected, each contact visit creates a timeline event on the HubSpot contact record. You can view all Vector activity for a contact directly inside HubSpot.

## In Salesforce
With the Salesforce integration, Vector creates activity records on the Lead or Contact object in Salesforce, so your sales reps have full context within their existing workflow.

## Alerting on Target Account Activity
Create a segment filtering for "company is in target account list" to receive real-time notifications when anyone from a target account visits your site.
