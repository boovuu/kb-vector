# Campaign Reporting Dashboard (Beta)

> Source: https://learn.vector.co/articles/4638323025-campaign-reporting-dashboard-beta

---

Vector's Campaign Reporting dashboard gives you contact-level attribution for your paid advertising campaigns.

## What Is Campaign Reporting?
Campaign Reporting connects your paid ad campaigns (LinkedIn, Google, Meta, etc.) to individual contact visits on your website. Instead of just seeing aggregate click data, you can see exactly which contacts visited your site from each campaign — with their full profile.

## How It Works
1. Your ad campaigns use UTM parameters
2. When a contact clicks your ad and visits your site, Vector captures the UTM data
3. Vector identifies the contact using its identity graph
4. The contact's visit is attributed to the campaign based on the UTM source and campaign values
5. All attributed visits appear in the Campaign Reporting dashboard

## Dashboard Views

### Campaign Summary
- Total contacts attributed per campaign
- Unique companies represented
- Visit timeline
- Top job titles and companies engaging with each campaign

### Contact-Level Detail
Drill down into any campaign to see every individual contact who visited from that campaign, with their full profile and the specific pages they viewed.

## Requirements
- Vector Pixel installed
- UTM parameters on all ad destination URLs
- Ads running on supported platforms (LinkedIn, Google, Meta, X, Reddit, Snapchat, TikTok)
