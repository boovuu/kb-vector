# Using Ad Platforms with Vector

> Source: https://learn.vector.co/articles/5614506055-using-ad-platforms-with-vector

---

Vector integrates with major ad platforms to let you sync your intent-based audiences directly to your advertising accounts for targeting and retargeting.

## Supported Ad Platforms

### Native Integrations (Automatic Daily Sync)
- **LinkedIn Ads** — highest match rates for B2B (up to 90%)
- **Google Ads** — Customer Match, works across Search, Display, YouTube, Gmail
- **Meta Ads** — Facebook and Instagram Custom Audiences
- **Reddit Ads** — Custom Audiences
- **X Ads** (Twitter) — Tailored Audiences

### CSV Export Integrations (Manual Upload)
- **Snapchat Ads** — upload CSV to Customer List
- **TikTok Ads** — upload CSV to Custom Audience

## How Audience Syncing Works
1. You create a segment in Vector with your desired criteria
2. You connect the segment to one or more ad platforms
3. Vector exports the contacts from that segment to the ad platform daily
4. Your audience in the ad platform stays fresh automatically

## Match Rates by Platform
| Platform | Typical Match Rate |
|---|---|
| LinkedIn | Up to 90% |
| Google Ads | Up to 45% |
| Meta Ads | Up to 45% |
| Reddit Ads | Up to 30% |
| X Ads | Up to 30% |
| Snapchat | Varies (lower for B2B) |
| TikTok | Varies (lower for B2B) |

## Use Cases
- **Retargeting**: Serve ads to contacts who visited your site but didn't convert
- **Lookalike expansion**: Use your best segments as seed audiences for lookalike targeting
- **Account-based advertising**: Target specific companies or contacts from your account list
- **Exclusions**: Exclude existing customers from prospecting campaigns
